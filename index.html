Who I Am

I’m a Senior Data Platform Engineer specializing in building scalable data platforms and cloud-native data infrastructure.

My work focuses on designing and implementing reliable systems that power modern data and analytics platforms. I enjoy working across the full data stack — from ingestion pipelines and orchestration to cloud infrastructure, storage layers, and analytics-ready data models.

I have extensive experience building AWS-based data platforms, including lakehouse architectures, ingestion frameworks, orchestration workflows, and production-grade data pipelines that support both operational and analytical workloads.

I’m particularly interested in building robust data platforms that combine software engineering principles with modern data infrastructure, enabling faster and more reliable access to trusted data.
